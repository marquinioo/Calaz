OUR STORY

More Than Just Tees

Founded in 2020, Calaz was born from a simple idea: create premium quality tees that don't compromise on style or sustainability.

Every piece in our collection is designed in-house and manufactured in small batches at our partner facility in Portugal. We work exclusively with organic cotton and sustainable materials.

From our first collection to the global community we've built today, our mission remains the same: empower individuals to express themselves through clothing that's made to last.
